About Me

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at the Georgia Institute of Technology advised by Prof. Juba Ziani. Before joining Georgia Tech, I completed my Masters in Computer Science at École Normale Supérieure de Lyon and my Bachelors in Computer Science at the Birla Institute of Technology and Science

I'm passionate about Machine learning systems that make good sequential decisions, preserve privacy and are fair to subpopulations; my research is in Sequential Decision Making, Differential privacy and Fairness in Machine learning.

In my free time, I enjoy climbing 🧗, hiking and board games.
